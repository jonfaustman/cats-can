What is Cat's Can!?

Cat's Can! is a supporting organization*, whose intended goal is to support local charities while supporting the Earth through recycling.

Aluminum cans are collected and recycled at the current return rate, with all proceeds going towards a local charity.

Local charities will be selected and will change each collection period (based on time and/or monetary amount.)

Why Cat's Can!?

Recycling is important. Supporting local charities is important. Combining the two is simply logical.

How can you help Cat's Can!?

Individuals

Simple! Save your aluminum cans, bring them to us, or arrange for a pickup.

Businesses

Simple! Save your aluminum cans, bring them to us, or arrange for a pickup.

If a new container for recycling would further the idea within your business, we can discuss a material donation. In other words, Cat's Can! will purchase a container for you, with your committment to participate.

Charities

Simple! Save your aluminum cans, bring them to us, or arrange for a pickup.

If you want to be added to our list of local charities, please contact us.

Hopefully you've noticed that helping Cat's Can! is simple. You can also help by spreading the word.

When can you help Cat's Can!?

Right now! Well, maybe tomorrow - the point is to get involved soon. More people involved means more aluminum cans; more cans means more money for local charities; more means money more support.

Who runs Cat's Can!?

Cat's Can! is the brainchild of Jon Faustman. Upon seeing an abundant amount of Tallgrass cans in one of Howie's existing charity bins, collecting aluminum cans from local businesses for local charities seemed like a foregone conclusion.

*Cat's Can! is not yet an IRS recognized supporting organization.

Cat's Can! is an independent supporting organization and is not affiliated with Kansas State University.
